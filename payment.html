Payment method

In case you are not able to visit Shimmer store for some of personal reasons, you can order products online on Facebook, Email and this official website. By the way, you should notice that Shimmer not allow to phone and message orders.

Easy way for customers living in Saigon: your side – ordering by methods above and our side – confirming the orders. When the process is completed, there are two available choices for customers to receive the products

- Come to Shimmer store to receive.

- Shimmer will ship products to customers’ address inner city (the cost is 20,000 VND per shipping for center districts and 30,000 VND for far districts) and receive directly.

No deposit refunds when the orders be canceled

We reserve the right to refuse to exchange or refund products that are not comply with our policy.
