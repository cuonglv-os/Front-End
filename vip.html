VIP

1. Conditions to Apply

1.1 Conditions to Participate

Customers with payments in 2017 that add up to VND 6,000,000 will be upgraded to Shimmer VIP.

Shimmer VIP with total payment in 2017 that add up to VND 10,000,000 will be upgraded to Shimmer VVIP.

1.2 Conditions to Apply

Exclusive to Shimmer VIPs and VVIPs.

Applicants are required to present VIP/VVIP member cards before payment to enjoy our discounts at all Shimmer showrooms.

*VIP/VVIP cards are not applicable for online purchases or together with other promotions.

1.3 VIP/VVIP Clients’ Benefits

Shimmer VIP/VVIP will be offered favorable promotions as followed:

· 10% discount on total payment for VIP card.

· 15% discount on total payment for VVIP card.

· Gain access to exclusive VIP/VVIP programs.

2. Duration of Usage

VIP/VVIP card holders enjoys a 12-months membership since card issuance date. VVIP cards after expiration will be offered VIP card benefits for the next 12 months.

3. Terms of Service for VIP/VVIP

VIP/VVIP cards will be issued for free of charge on the first time. In case of missing, please contact Shimmer to receive another at no additional cost (1 time only).

Upon expiration, VVIP customers please bring your cards to Shimmer to exchange for your new VIP cards and activate benefits.

The activation of VIP/VVIP cards means that you have accept Shimmer’s conditions terms of service. Shimmer is rightful to make changes to the conditions and benefits of VIP/VVIP membership without foretold announcement prior to adjustments.
