Shipping & Return

No cash refunds are permitted on purchased products.

You can exchange purchased products within 3 days (from the billing date) include your bill, for the same or higher value products.

No return/exchange on the following:

  • - No bill include.
  • - Producs purchased over 3 days. In this case, ring size's adjustment will be charged.
  • - Broken products (please check the items carefully before purchase).
  • - Customised products, customised size (standard size: ring 1.4- 1.8, bangle: 5.2- 5.6). Adjustment fees will be charged.

No deposit refunds when the orders be canceled

We reserve the right to refuse to exchange or refund products that are not comply with our policy.
